Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Simply browse our products, add items to your cart, and click "Checkout via WhatsApp". Your order will be sent directly to us on WhatsApp!

How do payments work?

After you send your order via WhatsApp, our shopper will prepare your bill. Payment details will be shared separately - no online payment required on this website!

We currently serve Mumbai and surrounding areas. Contact us on WhatsApp to check if we deliver to your location.
